  • Data Flux

  • The moving dots on the map represent the flow of people on a day of the week. It is then possible to deduce behaviors and habits that are then analyzed to develop new services for citizens.

  • Tomorrow, every citizen will be connected. It will then be possible to collect the information that each share publicly. The city can therefore capture these data to fit the citizen in real time. Where can I find a free place to my car in my neighborhood? Will the Poste be crowded at this time? (La Poste,, French mail service) Which mean of transportation can I use today to get to work? These questions will be answered through the sharing of data and information.
